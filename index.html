About Me

My research intersects robotics, user experience design, and artificial intelligence. I develop and evaluate innovative user interfaces that enable seamless human-robot task communication, ultimately empowering non-experts to create personalized robotic applications.

This fall, I will be joining the Computer Science Department at George Mason University as an assistant professor. I am looking for Ph.D. students!
